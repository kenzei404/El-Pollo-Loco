How to Play

Pepe, a Mexican chicken vendor, lived a peaceful life until one day the chickens had enough. They rebelled against Pepe, leaving him in a difficult situation. Now it's up to you to help Pepe navigate through the chaos and restore balance.

Your Goal:

  • Collect coins to maximize your progress.
  • Gather bottles to use as weapons.
  • Avoid enemy chickens or defeat them with skilled jumps and bottle throws.
  • Face the dreaded end boss at the end and defeat it to win the game!

Good luck, Pepe! The chickens won't make it easy, so stay alert and make the most of everything you find along the way.
